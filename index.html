Warranty Conditions

VEHICLE OWNER’S RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Vehicle Owner’s must properly use, maintain and care for the vehicle as outlined in the Owner’s Manual and determined by Matrix
  • Owner must service vehicle according to a Matrix accepted service schedule
  • Owner must maintain all service records of work performed to vehicle
  • Only an authorized Matrix dealer or service centre can perform warranty repairs
  • Periodic service and inspections are considered regular maintenance and not reimbursable
  • All warranty repairs require authorization by the Administrator
  • All Matrix products require an initial dealer pre-delivery inspection and set up to ensure proper operation and safety

LIMIT OF LIABILITY

The total amount of all benefits paid or payable under this warranty shall in no event exceed $5,000. Parts and labour expenses covered under this warranty shall be limited to the manufacturer suggested retail price for parts and labour allowances as approved by Matrix and the Administrator.

WARRANTY PERIODS

Warranty coverage begins on the date the vehicle is first sold to the consumer or the original in-service date and expires at the end of the term listed in Covered Parts below. Warranty coverage is automatically transferred to subsequent vehicle owners. Proof of purchase will be required for all warranty claims.

COVERED WARRANTY PARTS

This warranty covers the reasonable cost to repair defects in material or workmanship reported during the applicable warranty period and which occur under normal use:

  • Engine: 5 Years
  • Drivetrain Components: 5 Years
  • Exhaust System: 1 Year
  • Suspension/Steering: 1 Year
  • Wire Harness/E.C.U.: 1 Year
  • Clutch/CVT/Secondary Clutch: 180 days
  • Battery (when purchased with a new vehicle): 6 Months
